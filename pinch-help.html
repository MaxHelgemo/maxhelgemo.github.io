← back how to get your data
step 1

Open Instagram and tap the menu icon (≡) in the top right corner of your profile.

step 2

Go to Settings and activity, then tap Accounts Center.

step 3

Tap your Instagram profile.

step 4

Tap Your information and permissions.

step 5

Tap Export your information.

step 6

Tap Create export.

step 7

Tap Export to device.

step 8

Make sure date range is set to All time and format is HTML. Then tap Start export — Instagram will notify you by email when it's ready.

Once you get the email, download the zip file to your phone and come back here to load it in. That's it.
Instagram sometimes splits large exports into multiple zips if you have a lot of data. Load the first one in as normal, then tap + add zip in the top right corner of the explorer to load the rest.
